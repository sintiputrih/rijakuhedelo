    The Jonas Brothers are back, and they only have adorable things to say about their wives and each other.

    Related Story

    In their new interview for BAZAAR.com's digital cover, Nick, Joe, and Kevin Jonas all opened up about how much their lives have changed since the group disbanded in October 2013. At the time, Nick told People that they were "closing a chapter," and fans seem pretty elated that the next chapter has already begun, just six years later.

    One of the major changes that has taken place since the band broke up is that each of them is now married. Kevin tied the knot with Danielle Jonas in December 2009, but Nick and Joe's weddings were much more recent. While Joe married Game of Thrones star Sophie Turner in Las Vegas following the Billboard Awards, Nick's wedding to actress Priyanka Chopra was a multi-day affair that took place in India towards the end of 2018. And now, Nick has revealed his sweet nickname for Chopra.

    As reported in BAZAAR.com's digital cover story, Chopra is apparently called "Pri" by Nick, which is beyond adorable.

    Of the incredible love story he's had with "Pri" over the last year, Nick explained, "Certainly the person I ended up falling in love with and marrying is very famous and successful and all of that." He continued, "I couldn’t have predicted that would happen, that life would take me down that path. The fact that she’d been working in this business for as long as I have, we could relate on that. I’m grateful to have found the right person." I'm not swooning—you're swooning!
