    Melly is a vastly recognized rapper and singer. He is also a songwriter known for songs such as Suicidal, Murder on My Mind, and Mixed Personalities. Currently, he is signed to Young Thug’s record label YSL Records and 300 Entertainment.

    Most noteworthy, Melly’s music videos for singles, 4 Real, Butter Pecan, and Medium Fries, and amassed 26 million, 16 million, and 11 million respectively. Likewise, he released his first album I Am You in 2018.

    As a result, YNW’s album appeared number 192 on The Billboard 200 in 2019. Furthermore, he released We All Shine while he is still in prison, which consisted of 16 tracks. On the other hand, he featured Kanye West in a Music video for “Mixed Personalities”.

    It is known that Melly was denied release from prison in 2020 after he contracted Covid-19. Prior, he served a one-year sentence after being charged with firing his gun near a school. As a result, he wrote the song “Murder On My Mind” while behind bars.
