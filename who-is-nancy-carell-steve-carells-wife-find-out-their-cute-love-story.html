    Nancy Carell Bio

    Nancy Ellen Carell (Wells) was born in Cohasset, Massachusetts on July 19, 1966, into a family of 8, with her parents, Robert and Carol Wells. She attended Cohasset High School and then Boston College, where she was a member of the improv troupe My Mother’s Fleabag. Nancy graduated from college in 1988.

    Career

    Nancy began her comedy career as a member of The Second City comedy troupe. She featured on the television show, Saturday Night Live in the mid-nineties, from 1995 to 1996. During this period, she impersonated people such as Cokie Roberts, Diane Sawyer, Kathy Ireland, Martha Stewart, Sharon Stone. However, she is best known for her impersonation of CNN anchor Bobbie Battista.

    After her time on Saturday Night Live, Nancy became a correspondent for the satirical late-night news television program The Daily Show. She held this position from 1999 to 2002 and appeared in 90 episodes.

    Nancy Carell also acts in both film and television. In 2003, she acted as a flight attendant in the comedy film, Anger Management. His acting credits further include The 40-Year-Old Virgin as Health Clinic Counselor, Bridesmaids, Finding a Friend for Doomsday. She portrayed Helen Goode in the ABC animated series created by Judge Mike The Goode Family Carol Stills, real estate agent and ex-girlfriend of her husband’s character, Michael Scott on The Office

    She portrayed Katy Perry in 2 episodes of Angie Tribeca, the TBS comedy series she created with her husband, Steve. The series which debuted on January 17, 2016 also starred Rashida Jones.

    Their beautiful love story

    A marital union spanning more than two decades in an industry renowned for its rocky relationships is worthy of acclaim. It’s just a far cry from the pattern that’s been seen in Hollywood with celebrity marriages and relationships where separation and divorce are more like a default setting in such unions.

    This is not to say that marriage is problem free or has had its storm free sail, but it is more like a survivor amidst all the inconveniences and many challenges that such a union battles. Nancy Carell and Steve Carell have been married since 1995 and have remained one of show business’s strongest couples. Let’s take a look at their cute love story.

    How did they meet?

    The beautiful love story presented in the troupe, The second city where Nancy was a student in a class taught by Steve. However, contrary to what you might think, the two comedic forces didn’t get along at first, despite being attracted to each other. Steve revealed that asking her out was kind of a herculean task as he kept beating around the bush for a long time.

    He first thought that the lady he was interested in (Nancy) hated him because she acted calm around him even though he knew at first sight that she was his. But the truth was that she was just as nervous as he was and was just trying to act cool.

    Still, the thought of possibly being rejected brought to mind his memory of the first girl he called out on a date in eighth grade. He received a NO for an answer, and from that time until Nancy’s acceptance, was cautious in his journey.

    In any case, this was only the beginning of their happily ever after. They then won a date and had their first date at the Green Mill jazz club. The tandem bonded on August 5, 1995. The journey was characterized by endless happiness.
