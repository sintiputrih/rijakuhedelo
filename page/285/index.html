Who was James Herriot? Herriot was born in Sunderland on October 3, 1916 as James Alfred Wight, before moving to live in Glasgow just three weeks later. During his childhood, Herriot became captivated by the idea of treating sick animals as a career, so it was no surprise that he went on to become a fully qualified vet in 1939 after studying at Glasgow Veterinary College for six years.
