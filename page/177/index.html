Where is the most G-force on a roller coaster?

24 May 2024, 00:00

Roller coasters have been thrilling amusement park goers for decades, with their exhilarating drops, twists, and turns. One of the biggest factors contributing to the thrill of riding a roller coaster is the G-forces experienced during the ride. G-forces are the forces that act on a body as a result of acceleration or deceleration, and they can have a substantial impact on the human body. The most intense G-forces on a roller coaster are typically experienced during moments of rapid acceleration or deceleration, such as when the coaster is climbing uphill or plummeting down a steep drop.
