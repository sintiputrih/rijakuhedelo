Why does Tennessee have the Oilers logo?

06 Jul 2024, 00:00

Tennessee has the Oilers logo because the Tennessee Titans, the state’s NFL team, has a historical connection to the Houston Oilers. The Houston Oilers were a member of the AFL and then the NFL from 1960 to 1996. Owned by Bud Adams, the team had unique Columbia blue uniforms and a derrick logo that stood out as a unique brand during their time in Houston. In 1997, Adams relocated the team from Houston to Tennessee, where they were known as the Tennessee Oilers for two years before ultimately becoming the Tennessee Titans.
