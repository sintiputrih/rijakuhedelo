    Cenzatti is an Italian citizen who was born and raised in Pisa in the year 1969. Her actual birth date is still uncertain. Stay with us to know more information about her. 

    Failed Marriage With Ex-Husband, Andrea Bocelli

    The celebrity wife Enrica Cenzatti, gained the limelight after getting married to her now estranged husband, Andrea Bocelli. The couple first met each other at a piano bar when Enrica was just 17 years old.

    And it was love at first sight for Cenzatti. Andrea who is 12 years older than her, was working in local bars to support his studies at that time. After they soon started dating each other. Finally, the lovebirds exchanged wedding vows on 27 June 1992 in a private ceremony. 

    However, they dedicated not to continuing their marital life citing irreconcilable differences. They finalized their divorce in 2002 after being wedded for a decade. Since their separation, the ex-spouses' has been sharing a close and amicable relationship. She is known as a celebrity wife like Blanca Clemente.

    Two Children-Amos and Matteo Bocelli

    During their two years of nuptials, Cenzatti and her spouse, Bocelli shared two adolescent children. Both of them are sons. Their firstborn child, son Amos Bocelli, was born on 22 February 1995. He is currently in his late 20s. 

    Their youngest son, Matteo Bocelli was born on 8 October 1997. Matteo is a singer who has released several songs such as Fall On Me, and Can You Feel the Love Tonight. Even though the brothers live with their mother, they often visit their dad's house.

    As of today, despite their alliance ending, Enrica and the “Time to Say Goodbye” singer have remained on good terms and are raising their children together. They even live in the same neighborhood in Versilia. 

    Former Husband, Andrea's Second Eight Years of Conjugal Relationship

    Enrica Cenzatti is currently enjoying her singlehood as a single mother. On the other hand, her husband, Bocelli, the famous Amore singer went to marry his second wife and manager, Veronica Berti.

    Not to mention, the duo first met one another in 2002. After a long-term 12 years of love romance, they tied the knot on 21 March 2014 in a hush-hush wedding ceremony. Their big day took place in the coastal town of Livorno, Italy at the Sanctuary of Montenegro.

    The bride donned Ermanno Scervino's double silk dress and the groom wore a Corneliani suit. Before exchanging the wedding vows, Berti and Bocelli welcomed their first child, a daughter Virginia Bocelli on 21st March 2012. Today, the entire family resides in Forte Dei Marmi.
