    Keisha Grant Bio | Wiki

    Keisha Grant is an American journalist from West Hartford, Connecticut, United States. She currently serves as a TV News Anchor at WVIT (NBC Connecticut). Keisha has been with the channel for the last 18 years 9 months since Jan 2003. Her major role is to co-anchor the 6 and 11 pm newscasts.

    Prior to joining her current network, Keisha worked as a Weekend Anchor/Reporter in Columbus, Ohio (WSYX/ABC6) owned by Sinclair Broadcast Group. She served the network for 3 years 2 months from Nov 1999 until Dec 2002. Before that, Keisha worked in Palm Springs, California at
    KMIR 6 News. She served as the station’s General Assignment Reporter for 2 years 4 months from Jul 1997 until Oct 1999

    Keisha Grant NBC | Ct

    Currently, Keisha serves as an anchor for NBC Connecticut. She can be seen on weeknights at 5 p.m., 6 p.m. as well as 11 p.m. Keisha broadcasts the newscasts alongside her co-anchor called Mike Hydeck and First Alert Chief Meteorologist Ryan Hanrahan. The Emmy Award winner has covered a wide range of breaking news stories ranging from coast to coast. Also, she has covered some of Connecticut’s most memorable headlines of the last decade. Keisha has been honored with the NAACP award for her commitment to community involvement.

    Also, she holds awards from other organizations including MADD, The Society of Professional Journalists, and The Ohio Valley Chapter of The National Academy of Television Arts and Sciences, Most recently, Keisha won the Community Award. The award recognized her contribution to the field of journalism while she proudly represented her Bajan heritage.  The honor was bestowed upon her by the Consul General of Barbados in New York. During her free time, Keisha loves mentoring youth, hiking, traveling, and enjoying everything that nature has to offer.
