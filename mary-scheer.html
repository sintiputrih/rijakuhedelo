    Professional Life

    Mary Scheer started her career as an American Dubbing artist in the 1988 musical adventure film titled “The New Adventures of Pippi Longstocking”. After that, she voiced various characters in the video game titled “Lands of Lore Guardians of Destiny” in 1997. Moreover, she also lent her voice to various characters in animated series like “King of the Hill” and “Family Guy” in 1997 and 1999 respectively.

    After that, she appeared in various roles for the TV series “All That” from 1998 to 2005. She then appeared as Agent 2 in the film titled “Chump Change” in the year 2000.
