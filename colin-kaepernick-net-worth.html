    To begin with, his biological mother, Heidi Russo, was single and poor at the time she gave birth to Colin. Being eight years old he started to play football. Later, he successfully joined baseball and basketball teams.     Colin Kaepernick played college football for four years during which he is listed as receiving the following football awards: the Walter Camp Award, the Unitas Award, the Maxwell Award, the Paul Hornung Award, the Davey O’Brien Award and the Manning Award. With his hardest efforts, he made his dream come true, as he managed not only to play football but became one of the best players in college league.

In fact, being one of the best players in the league adds much financially to the total size of Colin Kaepernick’s net worth.
