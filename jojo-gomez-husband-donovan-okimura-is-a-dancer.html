    Donovan Okimura Is A Professional Dancer

    JoJo Gomez husband Donovan Okimura is a dancer and choreographer. He is one of the studio instructors at Millennium Dance Complex in Denver.

    He is also known by the nickname “The Rude Boy” and originally hails from Murrieta, California.

    He grew up training with the Temecula Dance Company under Erik Saradpon and was a part of the dance squad “FORMALity.”

    He received training in every type of dance, from hip-hop to ballet, for the competition scene. After that, he began taking the required actions to enter the professional industry.

    In 2012, Okimura appeared on live television for CBS ‘Paula Abduls: Live to Dance’ and Fox TV’s ‘Mobbed,’ from which his career took off.

    Later that year, he was granted the prestigious honor of receiving the top prize scholarship for the ‘Spotlight: Music Awards.’ He was also named a finalist for the Monster of Hip Hop Showcase.

    He completed high school in 2014 and was instantly scouted by Clear Talent Agency (CTG). He had received training from instructors like Brian Friedman, Kevin Maher, and CJ Salvador, to name a few.

    In 2015, he relocated to Los Angeles, where he assisted JoJo Gomez and Brinn Nicole. In the initial year, he participated in various live shows like ‘LA Fashion Week,’ ‘Phlextravaganza,’ and ‘Club Jete.’

    He has since become a showcase choreographer for ‘Club Jete.’ He also works as a studio instructor at Millennium Dance Complex, one of the world’s most recognizable dance studios.

    While he is not dancing, he enjoys photography. Okimura also often remains active on his social media handles to interact with his fans on Facebook, Instagram, and Twitter.

    His Instagram handle, under the username @rudekoji, has amassed approximately 71.8K followers so far. His dance and choreography videos, in which he collaborates with different dancers, are shown on the platform.
