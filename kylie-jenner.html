    Table of Biography

    Kylie Jenner is the youngest businesswoman, model, socialite, and social media personality who is famous worldwide. Kylie Jenner is regarded to be the youngest self-made billionaire making US $1 billion at twenty-one age.

    Early Life

    Kylie Kristen Jenner’s birthdate is 10th August 1997 and her birthplace is Los Angeles, California. She is twenty-six years old now and her zodiac sign is Leo. Likewise, the youngest billionaire holds an American nationality with mixed ethnicity.

    The name of her parents is Kris Jenner and Bruce who now is Caitlyn Jenner after going through a gender transition surgery. Both of them are TV personalities and her dad is a former Olympic gold medal-winning decathlete. Kylie is the youngest one in her family.

    From her father’s other relation, she has four half-siblings, Burt, Brandon, Brody, and Casey. And from her mother’s life, four siblings, Kourtney, Kim, Khloé Kardashian, and Rob, from her mother’s other relations.

    The famous model, Kendall Jenner is Kylie’s sibling. As for her education, she first went to Sierra Canyon School then after 2012, she was homeschooled. For her higher studies, she went to Laurel Springs School in Ojai, California, and graduated in July 2015.

    Kylie Jenner – Net Worth 2024

    As for her net worth, Kylie Jenner’s net worth was around US$1 billion as of 2019 at the age of twenty-one. This made her the world’s youngest and even self-made billionaire as of March 2019.

    In May 2020, Forbes brought out a declaration blaming Jenner for imitating tax documents so she would look to be a billionaire. Likewise, the publication blamed her for formulating revenue records for Kylie Cosmetics.

    Soon before her eighteenth birthday in 2015, she bought a 5-bedroom mansion worth $2.7 million in Calabasas, California.

    Body Measurements

    The amazing model has a very beautiful face and body structure. Kylie Jenner has a good height of 5 feet 6 inches or 168 cm or else 1.68 m. Similarly, she weighs about 63 kg or 139 pounds and she has a very curvy body type.

    As for her hair, it is black and she has a pair of beautiful brown eyes with a fair complexion. Jenner’s body measurements are 34, 24, and 39 inches, and her bra cup size is 32 C.

    The model before refuted receiving a lip augmentation. Moreover, there is no doubt that Kylie loves keeping herself healthy and fit together with her family.

    Career and Professional Life

    In 2007, Kylie Jenner began her televised career in her family’s famous reality TV series, “Keeping Up with the Kardashians”. She was present in other several spin-off shows concentrated on her siblings, Kourtney and Khloé.

    Likewise, her career as a model started in August 2010 by being featured in Sears’ “Crush Dream Commercial”. She also was gaining some interest in acting.

    At the Regency Village Theater, she became the host for “Glee: The 3D Concert Movie” together with her sister, Kendal. It happened on 6th August 2011, in Westwood, California.

    On 12th March 2012, they even interviewed the actors of The Hunger Games at the Los Angeles, California opening. Kylie made an impressive amount of money in 2011 endorsing Nicole’s OPI brand’s 2 nail lacquers.

    Their names were ‘Rainbow in the S-kylie’, and ‘Wear Something Spar-kylie’. In February 2013, she with her sister brought out “The Kendall & Kylie Collection” together with PacSun.

    They went on to bring their fashion line by Kendall & Kylie jewelry collection in June 2013 with Metal Haven, in association with Pascal Mouawad’s Glamhouse.

    More

    Similarly, they brought out a line of shoes and handbags in February 2014 for Steve Madden’s Madden Girl line. Kylie was also present in The Boy Band Project’s music video, “Find That Girl” in 2013.

    The businesswoman’s other music video presence includes Recognize and Come and See Me, Blue Ocean, Stimulated and Dope’d Up. She and her sister brought out the science fiction novel, “Rebels: City of Indra” in 2014.

    On 15 June 2014, Jenner acted for the first time in a promo for the Much Music Video Awards show. She and her sister were also the host of the same award show.

    Kylie independently brought out her hair extension line, “Kylie Hair Kouture” in 2015 with Bellami Hair. In March, she turned out to be the 2nd ambassador ever for the skincare line ‘Nip + Fab’.

    She again with Kendall brought out their clothing line, “Kendall + Kylie” in June 2015 with British fashion retailer, Topshop. In September, she made her website and a paid lifestyle app, with her lipstick line, “Kylie Lip Kit”.

    In 2016’s fall, she was the face of Puma. Additionally, the series, “Keeping Up” with the Kardashians produced great viewership even after getting criticized.

    Charity works

    Other than these, the young businesswoman has been active in numerous charity works as well. She with her siblings collected money for the Children’s Hospital Los Angeles in 2013 by auctioning old outfits on eBay.

    On 10 November 2013, the whole family organized a charity yard sale. Kylie even took part in a charity bowling game on 19th January 2014 at PINZ bowling alley in Studio City, California.

    This time, they collected money for The Robin Hood Foundation. She with Kendall took part in Chris Brown’s 2 Kick’n It For Charity Celebrity Kickball games.

    It was in July and August 2014 in Glendale, California. In the Teen Choice Awards of the year 2013, she shared the Choice TV Reality Star: Female award with her family for their reality television show, “Keeping Up with the Kardashians”.

    Likewise, the businesswoman was nominated for the Teen Choice Award several times. In the years 2014 and 2015, she and her sister, Kendall enlisted in Time magazine’s Most Influential Teens.

    Before the year 2011, they were also picked by Seventeen Magazine in the category of the Style Stars of the Year.

    Relationship Status

    It was informed by usmagazine.com in November 2011 that Kylie Jenner was in a relationship with an Australian pop singer. His name is Cody Simpson and she was just fourteen years old at that time.

    They met each other for the first time at Breaking Dawn: Part 1 opening in Hollywood. In September 2014, she started dating rapper Tyga who presented her in his music videos. However, they had a hard time in November 2015.

    But they got back together soon after but again got parted in March 2017. The couple reported in August 2015 that he gave her a white Ferrari 458 convertible worth $260,000 as a gift on her eighteenth birthday.

    On her next birthday, he gifted her a Mercedes-Benz Maybach. In 2016, the model got together with the rapper, PartyNextDoor after appearing in one of his music videos. She was also found to be comfortable with rapper Ty Dolla Sign in the same year.

    It was at the Nylon & BCBGeneration Young Hollywood Party. Since 2017, she has been in a relationship with Travis Scott with whom she has a daughter, Stormi Webster.

    Sadly, they got separated in 2019 but got back together after spending quarantine together during the coronavirus pandemic.

    On 7th September 2021, Kylie declared that she was pregnant with their 2nd kid. Happily, on 2nd February 2022, their son, Wolf Webster came into this world.
