    As Leah Messer stated during an October 2020 episode of Teen Mom 2, Royer couldn't attend the birth of his son due to the "immigration process," per In Touch Weekly. So Leah stepped up to support Victoria during labor, even sharing a photo on Instagram of herself in the delivery room. "We're so in love with you Caí River Rodríguez," Leah captioned the shot. "Im so proud of you sister!"

    Leah and Victoria have always been tight, with the reality star treating her little sis to the perks of starring on MTV. "So glad we enjoyed your first trip to NYC together!" Leah captioned an April 2019 pic with Victoria. "I hope it wasn't too busy and you had fun." Victoria seems to appreciate her sister's influence, writing alongside a snap of the two, "She keeps me safe, I keep her wild." Leah commented on the post, "This is a fact. I love you."

    It's not clear whether Royer will make it to the United States, but it's likely Leah will have Victoria's back no matter what. "I'm 100% stepping up to support my sister in Royer's absence," Leah said during a September 2020 episode of TM2. Talk about an unbreakable bond.
