    What happened to DJ Khaled? His surfing injury, explained

    Globally renowned legendary music producer, rapper, and DJ, DJ Khaled is trending on the internet after posting a video on social media detailing his recent surfing accident. The video shared on Instagram by the “Bugatti” music artist showed him enjoying surfing on a fine sunny day.

    Recommended Videos

    Cruising through the ocean with his electrical surfboard, Khaled seemingly had a good time, and 30 seconds into the video, we see him losing balance, trying to hold on to his board, and hitting the waters hard in what looks like a painful fall.

    Showcasing his jovial personality to fans and followers, Khaled slow-motioned and replayed the part when he fell in the video quite a couple of times to add humor to the whole incident. This isn’t the end as the Miami producer immediately visited a spa afterward to massage his pain away and briefly explained the incident.

    This he did by posting on Instagram captioning the video he posted of him lying on his face naked in a spa saying,

    “So, I’m getting a massage right now, because I hurt myself yesterday surfing. I have the footage of me falling…flying through the sky, hitting the board on my rib. Bellyflopping in the water.”

    Talking further on the subject, Khaled mentioned that the injuries he suffered are grave, which might cause him to seek medical advice. However, this hasn’t stopped him from doing what he does best — that is, engaging in exciting adventures and living his life to the fullest.

    The day immediately after the surfing disaster, Khaled went out golfing but unfortunately couldn’t continue with the endeavor. He later posted saying that it was the pain that caused him to go for the massage,

    “I’m in so much pain. I tried to golf this morning…I made it to like the eighth hole. Then I had to stop myself. So, I came to get a massage to get better.”

    Despite having met with a tragic, unexpected accident, it is Khaled’s optimism and energy which has elicited positive responses from his fans with everyone hoping for him to get better.
