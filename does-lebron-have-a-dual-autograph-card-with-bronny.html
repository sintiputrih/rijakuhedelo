    LeBron James and his elder son, Bronny, are set to appear on a unique 1-of-1 basketball card featuring dual autographs from the father-son pair. The card will include images of LeBron James in his St. Vincent-St. Mary's High School jersey and Bronny James in his USC Trojans jersey. This trading card will be released as part of the 2023-24 Bowman University Chrome Basketball set at the end of January.

    The Fanatics Deal

    LeBron James has signed a new multiyear trading card partnership with Fanatics Collectibles, and the dual autograph card featuring him and Bronny is part of this agreement. This marks a significant partnership for James, who previously worked with Upper Deck for his entire professional career. The announcement teased by James on his Instagram account earlier this week has garnered attention and excitement among fans.

    LeBron's Statement

    In a statement, LeBron James expressed his excitement about the partnership with Fanatics, emphasizing the importance of celebrating greatness in sports and sharing those moments with fans. He mentioned his appreciation for both big and small moments in the journey of athletes at the top of their games, highlighting the significance of celebrating the journey and enjoying those moments, with many more to come.

    Previous Card Auctions

    LeBron James' popularity in the trading card market is evident from previous auctions. A James "Triple Logoman" card from the 2020-21 Panini basketball card set sold at auction for $2.4 million in June 2022. Additionally, one of James' Upper Deck rookie cards, an autographed 2003-04 Exquisite Rookie Patch numbered 14-of-23, sold at auction for $1.8 million in June 2020. James has even revealed that he holds two of his own rookie cards, reflecting the value and significance of his trading cards in the collectors' market.

    The upcoming release of the dual autograph card featuring LeBron and Bronny James is a highly anticipated event for collectors and fans of the basketball stars. The partnership between LeBron James and Fanatics Collectibles signifies a new chapter in the trading card market, and the excitement surrounding this unique card is a testament to the enduring popularity and influence of LeBron James and his family in the sports memorabilia industry.
