    Ellen Pompeo reveals how she's really feeling after leaving Grey's Anatomy

    Ellen Pompeo is about to embark on a whole new chapter of her life and she's already got some surprising feelings about it. Grey's Anatomy has played a huge part in her and her family's existance over the years but now she's bid farewell - and she's loving it!

    23 Mar 2024, 00:00

    The mom-of-three recently revealed she was leaving after 19 seasons

      Ellen Pompeo is about to embark on a whole new chapter of her life and she's already got some surprising feelings about it. 

      Grey's Anatomy has played a huge part in her and her family's existance over the years but now she's bid farewell - and she's loving it!

      RELATED: Ellen Pompeo bids sad farewell to Grey's Anatomy after 19 seasons - 'I am eternally grateful'

      In an interview on , Ellen was asked how she's dealing with her emotions over the departure. 

      WATCH: Inside the home Ellen Pompeo shares with her husband and children

      Rather than be mixed or scared, she said: "I feel super happy, but listen the show has been incredible to me and I've loved a lot of the experience."

      She added: "Listen it's just I gotta mix it up a little bit. I'm 53, my brain is like scrambled eggs ... I gotta do something new or I'm literally gonna turn into, like, [someone who] can't do the New York Times crossword puzzle every single day."

      Ellen went on: "I mean ten years, that's more than people keep their kids in their house — like people keep their kids in their house until they're 18 and then they send them off to college, so this is like me, like, going away to college."

      Ellen is excited to spend more time with her husband and their children

      She said she's most looking forward to spending time with her children — son Eli Christopher, 5, and daughters Sienna May, 8, and Stella Luna, 13 — who she shares with her husband, Chris Avery.

      Ellen is also excited to shoot her upcoming Hulu limited series Orphan.

      "I have a lot that I'm doing," she shared. "I have three kids and so I take care of them, and it's really important for me to be around for them and be more present for them. I'm gonna do a limited series for Hulu in the spring, which is a very cool kind of crazy true story."
