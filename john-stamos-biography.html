    Who is John Stamos?

    John Stamos is an American actor and musician. He is extremely prominent for his role in ‘General Hospital’, ‘Dreams’ and ‘Grandfathered’.

    John Stamos: Age, Parents, Siblings, Ethnicity

    John was born on August 19, 1963, in Cypress, California, U.S.A. His nationality is American and belongs to Mixed (Greek, English, and Irish) ethnicity.

    He was born to William John Stamos (father) and Loretta Phillips (mother). He was raised up by his parents in the musical and entertaining environment along with his siblings Alaina Stamos and  Jansen Stamos.

    In which school did John Stamos attend?

    Regarding his educational background; he has earned a degree in arts and literature from John F. Kennedy High School.

    John Stamos: Professional Life, Career, and Awards

    John Stamos made his professional acting debut in the year 1982 and appeared in soap opera “General Hospital” for which he won two awards including ‘Young Artist Awards’ and ‘Soapy Awards’ in the category of ‘Outstanding Supporting Actor in a Drama Series’ and ‘Most Exciting New Actor’ respectively. Also, he was nominated for ‘Daytime Emmy Awards’ for the same drama series.

    John Stamos has made film debut in the year 1986 and appeared in “Never Too Young to Die” in a minor role as ‘Lance Stargrove’. John is also a fantastic drummer and guitarist. He released his first album titled “Shades of Blue” along with Lanny Cordola and Gary Griffin in 1994.

    John has also directed music videos titled “Let Yourself Be Loved.” He has also performed in a concert with ‘The Beach Boys’.  Some of his latest films include “My Big Fat Greek Wedding 2”, (2016), “They Came Together” (2014), “My Man Is a Loser” (2014).
