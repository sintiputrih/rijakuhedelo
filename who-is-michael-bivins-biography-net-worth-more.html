    Michael Bivins, who was born on August 10, 1968, in Boston, Massachusetts, USA, under the sign of Leo and with American citizenship, is well-known for his work as a rapper and vocalist. He is also a manager and a producer. The R&B music groups Bell Biv DeVoe and New Edition were founded by Michael and a few of his pals.

    How much money is Michael Bivins worth?

    Michael’s current net worth has been calculated to be more over $40 million, according to reliable sources.

    Michael Bivins childhood and education

    Since he hasn’t talked about having any siblings, most people assume Michael is an only child. Michael spent his whole life in Boston, but he hasn’t shared many details about his parents with the public because he values their privacy and doesn’t want them to be involved in the media.

    Michael grew up listening to R&B music, which served as a means for him to escape the harsh realities of life. He has frequently discussed how music helped him avoid trouble. Michael attended one of the neighborhood high schools in Boston, where he was reasonably well-liked because he was already producing his own music. After graduation, he made the decision to solely concentrate on his musical career and did not enroll in college.

    Michael Bivins Singing career and other projects

    With the aid of his pals, who together had formed the band New Edition in 1978, Michael began his career (at the age of 10). The band took some time to gain popularity, which peaked in 1983 when it consisted of Michael Bivins, Bobby Brown, Ricky Bell, Ronnie DeVoe, and Ralph Tresvant. In 1990, the group had a lengthy hiatus until coming back together in 1996. However, after their “Home Again” tour went awry, Michael permanently departed the group, which led to the dissolution of New Edition.

    So Michael formed the Bell Biv DeVoe band, which would surpass even New Edition in popularity, along with Ricky Bell and Ronnie DeVoe. They are credited with establishing the “new jack swing sound” with the release of their first album, “Poison,” which earned widespread acclaim and peaked at number five on the Billboard 200 list. The band is still going strong and has put out three more albums, although none of them compare to their first. Another Bad Creation, MC Brains, and 702 are just a few of the bands that Michael has worked with to locate members, develop their music, and create their voice, in addition to his own band. Michael is also a music manager.

    Michael has also dabbled in the movie business; he played the Heart Attack character in the movie “Crossover.” His other accomplishments include serving as the CEO of Sporty Rich Enterprises and approving the usage of his songs in the video game “Grand Theft Auto: San Andreas.”

    Michael Bivins Wife

    Since Michael tries to keep his family out of the spotlight, not much is known about his wife and kids. In 2008, he wed Teasha Bivins, whom he allegedly first met when they were both students at the same high school.

    When they first started dating, they took their time since they both wanted to focus on their professions before getting married. Michael Bivins Junior, Savi, Shi, and Star are the couple’s current four children; the dates of their birth are unknown. The family seems to be getting along well, and there haven’t been any rumors or issues around them yet.

    Teasha appears to be Michael’s lone love because he hasn’t mentioned any other ladies he might have been with. Teasha and Michael met in high school.
