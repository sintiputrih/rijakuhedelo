    Fann Wong Biography

    Fann Woon Fong, popularly known as Fann Wong, is a well-known and greatly admired Singaporean actress, singer, and model. She is referred to as MediaCorp’s “Ah Jie” (senior actress) together with Zoe Tay and Xiang Yun for being among the first locally trained artists.

    At Singapore’s Star Awards 1995, Wong became the first actress to win both the Best Actress and Best Newcomer awards in the same year. After gaining regional exposure and popularity in Asia through film and television productions, she became the first Singaporean actress to break into Hollywood, playing Chon Lin in the 2003 film Shanghai Knights.

    Wong was also the youngest actress to possess earned an All-Time Favourite Artiste at the Star Awards after winning the highest 10 hottest Female Artistes award from 1995 to 2004 respectively with Xie Shaoguang. She married actor Christopher Lee on 29 September 2009.

She continued to star during a large array of drama serials, including A Romance in Shanghai (produced in Shanghai), Wild Orchids (filmed in Sydney), and Brave New World (filmed in Amsterdam and Belgium).
