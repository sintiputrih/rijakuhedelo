    She won a Grammy Award with Carlos Santana in 2003 for Best Pop Collaboration with Vocals for her song, "Game of Love."

    She was married to her bass player Teddy Landau from 2004 to 2015; they had a daughter named Owen together. She began dating Patrick Carney of The Black Keys in 2015, and they got engaged in 2017. They announced they were expecting their first child together in February 2018.
