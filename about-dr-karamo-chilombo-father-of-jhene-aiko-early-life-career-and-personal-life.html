    Get to know more about Dr. Karamo Chilombo, including his background, profession, and personal life.

    Dr. Karamo Chilombo is a distinguished figure in the medical field, specializing in pediatrics and also pursuing a career in music under the stage name Dr. Chill. Originally named Gregory Wycliff Barnes, he hails from Zambia and later adopted the name Karamo Chilombo. With over four decades of experience, he has dedicated his expertise to the care of infants, young children, and adolescents.

    Biography & Profile Summary

    Ethnicity, Family, and Professional Career

    Dr. Karamo Chilombo, an American by origin, was born in Bakersfield, California. He is also recognized for his musical pursuits under the moniker ‘Dr. Chill Beams OG.’ Among his six daughters, Jhene Aiko and Mila J have made a name for themselves as esteemed singers. Karamo’s flair for fashion and personal style have also garnered attention, aligning with his dedication to staying updated on the latest trends. Jhene Aiko, born in 1988, has achieved significant acclaim as a Grammy-nominated vocalist and composer.

    Besides his medical profession, Dr. Karamo Chilombo is known for his musical endeavors, often referred to as “the singing doctor.” He has released several tracks on platforms like YouTube, Deezer, and Spotify, collaborating with his daughter Jhene Aiko on some of them. Notably, his son Jahi is the CEO and founder of EBF INC/Records Luxury Ambassador. With an ethnic background comprising African-American, German-Jewish, Spanish, Japanese, and Dominican heritage, Karamo is a multifaceted individual. He is also an author, with one of his notable works being ‘LOVE IS LIFE.’

    Marriage and Personal Life

    Karamo Chilombo shared a two-decade marriage with his ex-wife Christina Yamamoto, with whom he tied the knot in 1980. The union resulted in two sons and three daughters before they chose to part ways in 2000.

    Net Worth

    His accomplished career in medicine has contributed to Dr. Karamo Chilombo’s net worth estimated at approximately $150,000.

    • He has served in the pediatrics department for over four decades, specializing in the care of infants, toddlers, and teenagers.
    • Dr. Karamo Chilombo is renowned for his fashion sense and keenness for the latest trends.
