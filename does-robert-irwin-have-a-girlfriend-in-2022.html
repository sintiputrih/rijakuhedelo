    Some rumors claim that Robert Irwin and Emmy Perry have been dating for some time now. Although, both have not made it official.

    Their dating rumor had started in early May 2020 when the two had met at a Gala Dinner together in Los Angeles.

    On September 202, Irwin had wished Emmy a happy birthday on Instagram. After which, fans were quick to speculate that the wildlife conservationist has something with the former Glee star. He also shared a series of pictures of them together in Disneyland.

    Irwin wrote: “Happy birthday @emmyperryxo! We’re thinking of you today and sending koala cuddles from Australia Zoo! I hope it’s a wonderful celebration. Can’t wait for another Disney day with you guys when the world opens back up again!”

    In reply, Perry shared his post on her Instagram story with a GIF of Winnie-the-Pooh blowing a butterfly off his nose. 

    She commented: “Thank you so much! I can’t wait for the world to open up and get those koala cuddles in person! And there will be more Disney days for sure”

    Perry has also been toed with actor Jack Dylan Grazer. The child actress has been part of movies like Weiner Dogs International, Scales: Mermaids Are Real, and 26 Candles. On Television, she appeared as Youg Becky in the series Kismet.
