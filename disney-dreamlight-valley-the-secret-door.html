    The Secret Door quest is the final step to completing Mickey Mouse's quest in Disney Dreamlight Valley. Here's the location of the door.

    First Published September 22, 2022, 15:10

    There are plenty of realms and mysteries to uncover in Disney Dreamlight Valley, including The Secret Door quest. This challenge will have you solving riddles, through which you can learn more about Disney's famous Mickey Mouse character.

    However, figuring out where The Secret Door is located can be challenging. But don't worry. This article will detail its location and provide a step-by-step guide on how to complete Mickey's Final Friendship Quest in Disney Dreamlight Valley.

    Where to find the Secret Door in Disney Dreamlight Valley

    You can only begin The Secret Door quest can once you’ve raised Mickey’s Friendship to Level 10. After doing this, you will unlock this quest and must open a chest in your inventory. Once opened, you will find a Memory image showing the Secret Door covered partially by curtains. 

    During the quest, you will be notified to find and talk to Mickey. After you’re at his location on the map, Mickey will provide you with a riddle tasking you to find The Secret Door. 

    Make your way back to Dream Castle, and as you turn to the right, you will notice a purple curtain behind potted plants next to the stairs. Once you interact with this curtain, it will reveal The Secret Door in Disney Dreamlight Valley.

    How to unlock the Secret Door in Disney Dreamlight Valley

    Then, return to Mickey to receive the second riddle that unlocks The Secret Door. The puzzle explains that the door has no key and will require you to collect matching Gemstones.

    You will require Normal Gemstones to unlock The Secret Door in Disney Dreamlight Valley, which we've listed below.

    • Garnet
    • Aquamarine 
    • Citrine 
    • Tourmaline

    Once you’ve opened The Secret Door, interact with Mickey, and he will reward you with an item that represents a token of your friendship. And that concludes our guide on completing The Secret Door Quest in Disney Dreamlight Valley.
