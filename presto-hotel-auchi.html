    Boasting free Wi-Fi throughout the premises, Presto Hotels is located at 100, Warrake Road, Auchi, Edo, Nigeria.

    Each room is air-conditioned and equipped with a refrigerator, satellite TV and an en-suite bathroom with a water heater and toiletries. These rooms are categorised into Classic Double, Executive Room, King Size Executive and the Amira/Otaru Suite.

    Adequate parking is available on the premises, which is also guarded by security personnel. Access to a nightclub is also something guests can enjoy at the hotel. Various kinds of meals and drinks can be ordered from the on-site restaurant and bar.
