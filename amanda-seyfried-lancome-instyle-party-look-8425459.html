    Amanda Seyfried Wore Bright Berry Lipstick to the InStyle x Lancme Party

    06 May 2024, 00:00

    Whether it's a daring eye look or a fresh take on Old Hollywood curls, Amanda Seyfried always brings it on the beauty front. Following her color theory-inspired Golden Globes glam on Sunday — one of our standout makeup moments from the night — she served up yet another mood board-worthy look wearing an undone blowout and bright berry lip.

    On Jan. 10, The Dropout actress celebrated the announcement of Lancôme's new global ambassador, Joy Sunday, at the Park Lane Hotel in New York City. Fellow ambassador Isabella Rossellini, whose lipstick-matching leopard manicure was nothing short of iconic, also attended the cocktail party, as well as Busy Philipps, AnnaSophia Robb, RHONY’s Ubah Hassan, Avantika of the new Mean Girls reboot, and InStyle’s EIC Sally Holmes.

    Styled by longtime collaborator Elizabeth Stewart, Seyfried paired a Thom Browne shirtdress featuring a buttoned-up collar and satin finish with a shiny black blazer (also by Thom Browne). A few rings and a sparkly pair of pointed-toe black heels finished the look.

    If her outfit was a grown-up take on the "no pants" trend, Seyfried's beauty choices matched the ensemble's elegant vibe. For hair, stylist Renato Campora created the perfect undone blowout, leaving her hair in sleek, natural-looking waves. Celebrity makeup artist Genevieve Herr added a softly sculpted eye, rosy cheeks, brushed-up brows, and a bright berry lip (courtesy of Lancôme, of course) to complete the look.

    "I honestly feel so grateful to be among these women with this kind of platform," Seyfried previously told InStyle about her Lancôme ambassadorship. "Beyond skincare and beauty, Lancôme reflects back to us the importance of trusting ourselves and being honest about who we are and why we matter. And I love being able to channel that message in every campaign."
