    Anne De Zogheb Bio

    Anne De Zogheb Early Life and Modeling Career

    Born on October 2, 1942, in Alexandria, Egypt, Anne De Zogheb hailed from a diverse heritage—Lebanese from her father and English from her mother.     In 1960, fate intervened when Anne met Paul Anka in San Juan, Puerto Rico, during a photo shoot. Despite Eileen Ford, her guardian and agent, expressing reservations about their relationship, Anne and Paul’s connection deepened. They married on February 16, 1963, at Paris-Orly Airport, embarking on a journey that would span nearly four decades and bless them with five daughters.

However, as their family expanded, they decided to relocate to Carmel, California, in the mid-70s. Anne devoted herself to raising their five daughters—Amanda, Amelia, Alexandra, Anthea, and Alicia. Despite the challenges, the Anka family created enduring memories in their California home.
