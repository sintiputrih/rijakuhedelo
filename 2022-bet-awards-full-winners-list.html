    A look at “BET Awards 2023: Here Is The Full List Of Winners” The 2023 BET Awards results are in!

    Drake has received the most nominations at the ceremony this year, which was held on Sunday in Los Angeles. His seven nominations include those for best male hip-hop artist, Best Male R&B/pop Artist, and Best Group with 21 Savage. Along with Album of the Year, Viewer’s Choice Award, and Outstanding Collaboration for his song “Wait for U” with Future and Tems, Drake, 36, was also nominated.

    GloRilla, a BET Amplified Artist last year, is second to Drake with six nominations. The 23-year-old rapper gained notoriety last year with her smash song “F.N.F. (Let’s Go),” and she was nominated for best female hip-hop artist, best new artist, album of the year for Anyways, Life’s Great, Best Collaboration, and Video of the Year.

    Nikki Taylor, Teyana Taylor’s mother, accepted her daughter’s prize and even called her during the show to inform her that she had won video director of the year. There were two high-profile ties among the 15 non-televised awards: Beyoncé’s Renaissance and SZA’s SOS tied for album of the year, and Chris Brown and Usher tied for best male R&B/pop artist.
