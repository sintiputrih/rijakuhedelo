    Kayla Braxton Documents Experience Before Successful Surgery

    Kayla Braxton was wearing glasses on Friday Night SmackDown this week for a reason. She can’t see without assistance, and she couldn’t wear contacts for two weeks. That was the doctor’s requirement before getting Lasik.

    Braxton updated fans via her Instagram stories. She gave her followers a few videos to document her experience. She was a bit nervous, but Braxton was brave and followed through. Now she’s in shades and waiting for her new eyes to heal up.

    The Bump’s host said that she can’t wait to be Braxton 2020, Kayla 2.0 is on the way. She will also be able to see without contacts of glasses.

    You can check out the video below that starts last night after SmackDown. She seemed exhausted, but understandably so after a live SmackDown and taping Talking Smack to air the next morning.
