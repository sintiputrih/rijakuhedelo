    16 Jul 2024, 00:00

    Finally! After a three-year hiatus, Celebrity Big Brother season 3 is here. And this time we have an olympian in the house. Mirai Nagasu is the first American ladies’ single skater to ever land a triple axel at the Olympics. Her strategy this season would be to “communicate” with her “alliances” and let them know her game plan.

    But today, we aren’t discussing Mirai’s achievements or her time on Big Brother. Rather delve into her relationship history and what has become of her romance with Darian Weiss as this article proceeds.

    Is Mirai Nagasu Still Dating Darian Weiss?

    No, Mirai Nagasu is not dating Darian Weiss. The two split before 2020.

    Even the pictures of Darian from all of Mirai’s socials are deleted now. In case you missed it, Darian was the star of Mirai’s IG. The two often flaunted their trips to the beach or theme parks or dinner dates then.

    Likewise, Mirai has vanished from Darian’s socials as well. He often referred to the Olympian as his “queen” over IG and seemed proud to have her. “Well, my queen is 3 weeks away from going to her 2nd Winter Olympic Games and I couldn’t be more proud of her,” he once wrote over IG.

    But while they were in relation, no one ever in their wildest dreams imagined that the lovebirds would split. Back when Mirai was all prepared to represent the US in the 2018 Olympics, she even started a YouCaring campaign (which raised over $18,000), so that her then-boyfriend and his family could afford to make it to the Olympics in Pyeongchang.

    Guess, nothing is evergreen.

    For those of you wanting to know more about Mirai’s ex-boyfriend, Darian Weiss is an actor and ice dancer who has forged a long television career. Surprisingly, the actor got his start in show business by appearing in a Carnation Good Start commercial when he was just 6 months old.

    Darian then went on to appear on movies and TV shows like Return to Innocence (2001), Days of Our Lives (2002), Miracle at Sage Creek (2005), In Gayle We Trust (2009), Good Luck Charlie (2011), Ditch Party (2016), and many more.

    He’s also skated alongside Olympics legends such as Scott Hamilton, Tai Babilonia, Kurt Browning, Kristi Yamaguchi, Ekatarina Gordaeeva, and Tara Lipinski.

    Did you know: Mirai met Darian after moving to Colorado for college.

    Was Mirai Nagasu Engaged To Her Then-Boyfriend Darian Weiss?

    It’s unclear if Mirai Nagasu and her then-boyfriend Darian Weiss ever got engaged. But Darian sure went on to get to bend the knee for his next girlfriend named Juliette Weiss on December 31, 2020.

    The pair held their wedding on July 13, 2021, after posting their wishlist on “registry.theknot.com” and wedding donations on “honeyfund.com.”

    It seems that Darian and Juliette were in a relationship ever since June 2019.

    Darian’s wife, Juliette is also a figure skating expert from Upland, California.

    Here’s her IG @julietteweiss_, and @foreverweiss.

    Mirai Nagasu Relationship Status

    After her split with Darian, Mirai Nagasu also went on to find love again with her boyfriend Michael Bramante. The two were together at least since June 2019 and were happily coupled up as of 2022.

    According to Michael, living with Darian was “exhausting!” He shared that during the lockdown the Olympian wanted to go work out four times a day. “And your legs are dead and your back is tired and she’s like ‘what are you complaining about, you didn’t even do half the workout,’” he sighed.

    But that wasn’t all, after the workout, the pair even went for a run of at least a mile or two.

    As for their time inside the home, it was spent binge-watching series and improving their culinary skills. Since Mirai was busy skating all these years, she never had time to follow her inherited passion for cooking. So, the 2020 lockdown was a perfect opportunity for her.

    Also, the duo celebrated their birthdays together during quarantine. It seems that Michael arranged a cinema date, just for the two of them, for Mirai’s turning-27 celebration.

    How? Well, Michael’s parents ran a movie theater in Belmont.

    The lovebirds are a year apart in age.

    The last we saw them over Mirai’s IG @mirainagasu was in Dec 2021, celebrating Christmas together.

    Fun Fact: Michael once received a haircut from Mirai’s mother (who’s a chef by profession).

    Who Is Mirai Nagasu Boyfriend?

    Mirai Nagasu’s boyfriend, Michael Bramante is a former U.S. and French ice dance competitor-turned-coach who was involved as a staff coach at The Skating Club of Boston. 

    His specialty includes — Ice Dance, Moves in the Field, and Stroking/Skating Skills.

    The guy has had 8  U.S. Championships titles under his name and is even a  National Novice Ice Dance bronze medalist alongside former partner Kaitlin Hawayek.

    Furthermore, this talented skater went on to compete for France and brought home the bronze medal at the 2015 and 2016 French National Championships, earning him a spot to compete for France in the 2016 European Championships as well.

    As for his education, Michael has a B.S. in Business Administration, a Certificate in Digital Marketing, and an A.S in Administration, all from New England College of Business.

    Here’s his Facebook @michael.bramante.5.
