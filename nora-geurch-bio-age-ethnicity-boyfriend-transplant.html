    From Nora Geurch’s Transplant stint to her age and career — this wiki-article has it all.

    Nora Geurch On Transplant

    Transparent tells us the story of Dr. Bashir Hamed, a Syrian Civil War refugee and a talented doctor, who fled his country with his younger sister Amira to pursue a fresh start in Canada. The Canadian medical drama walks us through the obstacles Dr. Bashir Ahmed aka Bash goes through to resume his career in medicine at York Memorial Hospital in Toronto.

    Nora was introduced to the Transplant viewers in the Season 1 finale. As Bashir and Amira were leaving, viewers saw a woman standing at the front desk of the hospital. Recognizing the woman, Amira ran screaming into her arms. Bashir simply looked at her and said, “I thought you were dead.” Viewers then learned the mystery woman’s name was Rania.

    Since Bashir had not seen or heard from Rania since he escaped Syria, seeing her stand there was a moment of shock for him. Things quickly escalated when Rania told Bash that she was aware of where he and his sister Amira were the entire time.

    According to E-News, Rania is Bashira’s fiancee and Bash had left her in Syria after he fled to Canada with his sister Amira.

    Rania announced her part in Transplant only on January 3, 2022, through her Instagram handle.

    Nora Geurch Age

    Nora Geurch was reportedly born in the year 1986, which means her age was either 35 or 32 as of March 2022.

    What Is Nora Geurch Ethnicity?

    Nora Geurch’s ethnicity is Moroccan-Canadian.

    To talk a little about her family, we know that her mom’s name is Khadija Gue. We also know that she completed her education at the University of Montreal and is a painter by profession. We, unfortunately, don’t have an inkling about Nora’s dad.

    Transplant actress Nora also has two siblings, one twin brother named Adil Geurch and the other a sister named Nadia Geurch.

    According to Nadia’s LinkedIn profile, she currently works for Mennonite Economic Development Associates as its Senior Regional Director of Global Programs. Nadia has also worked for Youth Challenge International and International Youth Foundation in the past.

    Some of Nora’s relatives are Myriam, Najib, and Bouchra Geurch.

    Does Nora Geurch Have A Boyfriend?

    Unfortunately, Nora Geurch has never talked about her love life in public. That is why it’s tough to answer if Nora has a boyfriend.

    Nora Geurch Career

    Actress Nora Geurch did her undergraduate studies at Montreal University. She studied Comparative Literature and Film Studies there from 2008 to 2010. She was also enrolled in the dramatic arts program at the University of Quebec in Montreal from 2010 to 2013.

    Upon her graduation from the University of Quebec in Montreal, she started working in plays, both in French and Arabic language. One of her first performances came in Joyce Carole Oates’s The Ontological Proof of Her Existence, in which she played Daniela.

    In 2016, Nora played Daniela in the Centaur Theatre production of BUS STOPS, and in 2018, she performed in The Assembly at Theatre ESPACE GO. Nora also performed in Antioch at Talisman Theatre. Antioch was a story of two women–a teenager, Lily, and her emotionally distant mother, Inas.

    Nora made a debut on the small screen in 2016 through a TV show named Blue Moon. She played the role of Laurence Vinet-Guillot in that TV series. In the same year, she was also part of another TV show named Les Simone. As of this writing, she has guest-starred on shows like The Bold Type and Jack Ryan.

    Nora has also acted in movies such as Zoe, Frames, and Death of a Ladies’ Man.

    Furthermore, from what we can tell, Nora is an excellent poet as well. You can find many of her open mic poetry sessions on her Facebook.
