    What Happened To The News Anchor Lan Kiese? Is He Leaving KPIX?

    Len Kiese celebrated the last day of his job at KPIX on Thursday. His co-hosts and colleagues gave him an emotional farewell while paying tribute to him. In addition, they telecasted clips from his days at KPIX and the memories he made with the people in the network.

    22 Jun 2024, 00:00

    Len Kiese celebrated the last day of his job at KPIX on Thursday. His co-hosts and colleagues gave him an emotional farewell while paying tribute to him. In addition, they telecasted clips from his days at KPIX and the memories he made with the people in the network.

    Tears rolled down his eyes when the channel paid him tribute. He was emotional to bidding goodbye to everyone and expressed how happy he had been to work there and find a family there.

    The Baltimore-born anchor got his first on-air position at WALB-TV in Albany, Georgia, a few months before receiving his degree from Towson University. He began as a reporter before being elevated to Saturday evening anchor.

    He relocated to Jacksonville, Florida, to take up a different reporting position at First Coast News. He also worked for WIS Sunrise as one of the weekday morning hosts for several years in Columbia, South Carolina, as per CBS News. 

    Then, he was appointed the morning and noon anchor for KPIX 5 News after spending almost four years in Orlando. He resides in the East Bay, which has been a home to him.

    He is an award-winning journalist with the title of Anchor of the Year by the South Carolina Broadcasters Association. During his time at WIS Sunrise, the morning team also won an Emmy. Thus, he has achieved success in his career throughout these years and is loved by many audiences.
