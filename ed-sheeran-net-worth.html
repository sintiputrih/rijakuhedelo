    He is also a rapper and an actor. His reputation skyrocketed with the publication of his album, Plus, as well as a guest appearance on Taylor Swift’s fourth studio album, Red. 

    In 2004, His debut album, Spinning Man, was self-released.

    Sheeran won the Brit Award for best songwriter of the year 2022. Sheeran was honored for his efforts on his own record, as well as composing credits for musicians such as Taylor Swift and BTS throughout 2021, and he praised his label, management, and his wife in particular.
