    Shopping for bridesmaids' dresses isn't easy, and trying to find a dress colour that everyone in the bridal party is happy with can sometimes be more challenging than finding the dress itself.

    In some cases, brides may choose the dress for the bridesmaids, in other cases, the bridesmaids may have free rein on what they can wear. Either way, finding the perfect dress the bridal party will feel comfortable in all day that complements the bride's dress as well as the wedding theme, can be difficult. However, we are here to help.  

    Pink has without a doubt been the colour of 2023 thanks to the Barbie movie, and as a result we're expecting to see even more pink bridesmaid dresses. Dusty pink, blush pink, baby pink and hot pink are all great shade options for bridesmaid dresses - and there's something to suit all skin and hair colours. 

    How to style a pink bridesmaid dress

    Thankfully, most shades of pink are super versatile, making it easy to style a pink bridesmaid dress. If the bridal party are wearing heels for the big day, white is a common option, or if you want to go bold, why not try a lilac or an orange to inject some added colour into the wedding photos. If flat shoes are on the cards, a pair of pearl white ballet flats would look lovely with a pink bridesmaid dress.
