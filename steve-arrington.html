    Biography

    Steve Arrington was born in Ohio on March 5, 1956. Ohio-born R&B singer known as the leader of Steve Arrington’s Hall of Fame and for solo albums like 1985’s Dancin’ in the Key of Life. He made a comeback in 2013 with the Dam-Funk collaborative album Higher. His song “Weak At The Knees” has been sampled by some of the world’s most famous rappers, including Jay Z. He was a member of the lounge band The Murphy’s in 1975 before moving to San Francisco.
