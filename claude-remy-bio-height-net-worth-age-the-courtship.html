    Claude Rémy Wife And Kids

    Dr. Claire Spain-Remy is the wife of Claude Remy. Mr. Remy met his wife in St. Louis at Washington University on 22 August 1980. Eventually, they started dating each other, got engaged, and married on 12 October 1985, 5 years after meeting each other.

    As of March 2022, Claude and Claire have been happily married for 36 years. Besides a successful marriage, they also have an equally successful professional life. His wife Claire graduated from Duke University Medical School in 1985. She completed her OB-GYN residency at Madigan Army Medical Center in Tacoma WA.

    Claire spent 12 years in private practice in Tacoma, WA, followed by the role of Specialty Medical Director for MultiCare Health system, then a 5 hospital healthcare system in the South Puget Sound of Washington. She has served as a healthcare consultant with ValueIQ. She serves on the boards of PacificSource Health Plans, the YMCA of Pierce and Kitsap Counties, the Tacoma/Pierce County chapter of Habitat for Humanity and is a member of the Duke Medical Alumni Council.
