    Buckle up—Julia Fox's latest street style look is a wild one.

    The Uncut Gems star continued her streak of belted looks today while out and about for Milan Fashion Week. Fox stepped out wearing an entirely white motorcycle jacket with a belt buckle at the collar. Underneath, she wore a cropped white top with a lace ruffle trim at the hem.

    Her unconventional miniskirt consisted of various leather belts in different shades of brown seemingly sewn together; the low-rise piece hung low on her hips, a silhouette that Fox is known to occasionally favor.

    She paired the statement skirt with statement boots, opting to wear slouchy denim over-the-knee stiletto boots. The top of the towering footwear nearly brushed the hem of her belt bottoms.

    Finally, she finished off the outfit with a white box bag slung over one shoulder.

    Rachpoot/Bauer-Griffin//Getty Images

    Rachpoot/Bauer-Griffin//Getty Images

    Earlier this week, Fox wore a similar look revolving around the versatility of belts.

    During a late night outing in Milan, she looked edgy in three thin belts strapped around her bust, forming a pseudo-bandeau bra, and again wore stacked belts as a micro miniskirt. The belts came in different textures and colors, from distressed olive green, bright red leather, and metallic silver to glossy black and a graphic print of a mouth opened wide. She capped off the unusual look with an oversized black trench coat, brown leather knee-high stiletto boots, and a croc-embossed bag from Diesel.

    Fox previously talked to The Cut about her experimental sense of style.

    "[Her publicity team was] like, 'Oh, they're a little too editorial,''It's too sexy. It's too provocative. It's too out there,'" she shared, adding that her tune has since changed. "I was just like, 'Okay, I guess I just have to be more Hollywood and more bland.' But fuck that narrative if I want to pop out and wear crazy shit—which is what I want to wear."
