    From Bulbapedia, the community-driven Pokémon encyclopedia.

    Laser Focus (Japanese: とぎすます Hone) is a non-damaging Normal-type move introduced in Generation VII.

    Effect

    Laser Focus causes the user's move to result in a critical hit until the end of the next turn, unless that move's target has Battle Armor, Shell Armor, or is under the effect of Lucky Chant.

    The effect of Laser Focus can be copied by Psych Up or Transform.

    If powered up by a Normalium Z into Z-Laser Focus, the user's Attack stat rises by one stage.

    Generation IX

    Laser Focus cannot be selected in a battle.

    Pokémon UNITE

    In Pokémon UNITE, Laser Focus is Duraludon's first move. The move reduces damage received by 20% for .5s. Afterward, the user's basic attacks deal additional damage to enemies hit. This buff expires after 8s or after 3 basic attacks. After reaching level 5, this move is replaced by either Flash Cannon or Dragon Pulse.
