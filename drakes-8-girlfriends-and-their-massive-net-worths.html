    Producer and rapper Drake entered the world of Hollywood through a Canadian teen drama when he was fifteen years old. Struggling to put money on the table, he got inspired by rap icons like Jay-Z to pursue music. After releasing mixtapes that were a moderate success, Lil Wayne signed him onto his record label, and his debut album was released in 2010, which took very little time to climb the top spot. All of Drake’s albums have been four times platinum, and he has become one of the most potent forces in hip-hop. His music, ventures, and endorsements have earned him a $250 million fortune. As a high profile, there is deep scrutiny regarding his personal life. However, Drake tries to remain as low-key as possible.

    From dating models to the biggest musicians in the industry, Drake has had an exciting love life that fans often get to hear about from his music and performances. From former flames during the early years of his career to superstars he dated during his peak, let’s take a look at the girlfriends Drake has dated and their massive net worths.

    8 Sophie Brussaux ($1 million)

    Drake’s baby mama, people were unaware of their short-term relationship until it was revealed in 2020 that Sophie Brussaux gave birth to their son in 2017. Drake took Instagram to release the first photo of his son Adonis. Brussaux, who was a former model, is also a world-traveling painter. The French artist has made portraits of Barack Obama, Frida Kahlo, Amy Winehouse, and even met Pope Francis to deliver his custom art.

    7 Johanna Leia ($1 million)

    Drake’s last known girlfriend, he dated Los Angeles-based model Johanna Leia in July 2021. The rumors first made rounds after they were spotted at a basketball game together, and Drake even rented out the Los Angeles Dodgers stadium for an intimate date night. Along with being a model, Leia is a reality show personality and has starred in Bringing Up Ballers. She has also worked with Fashion Nova to continue her modeling career.

    6 Catya Washington ($1.5 million)

    Drake and Catya Washington dated during the early years of his career from 2009 to 2010. They began seeing each other after she starred in his music video, Best I Ever Had, and the relationship featured date nights and family dinners; however, Washington decided to call it quits after he went on long trips to different cities and was spotted getting cozy with other women. Washington is a reality show star who participated in the Oxygen network’s Bad Girls Club.

    5 Imaan Hammam ($1.5 million)

    In early 2020 before the pandemic, Drake was spotted going out with Imaan Hammam all over New York, and they sparked rumors after he was spotted leaving her apartment early in the morning. Hammam is a Moroccan-Egyptian model who has walked the NYFW runway for Anna Sui. The relationship fizzled out within a few months, and Drake blocked her from social media.

    RELATED: Drake's 10 Most Popular Songs (According To Spotify)

    4 Bella Harris ($4 million)

    Drake and Bella Harris were spotted grabbing dinner in September 2018, but the rumors of their dating began in the summer. The fling didn’t last long, and they parted ways soon after. Harris signed up with Elite Model Management the same year and has walked the runway for several designers during the New York Fashion Week. Harris is also the daughter of legendary music producer Jimmy Jam.

    3 Blac Chyna ($5 million)

    First linked to Blac Chyna in 2014, Drake first made rumors a reality when he got into a fight with her then-boyfriend Tyga. To add more fuel, Chyna posted an explicit picture of herself and tagged Drake with a caption for him. Drake and Chyna had also cozied up on various occasions after she broke things off with Robert Kardashian.

    2 Jennifer Lopez ($400 million)

    A rumor started with an Instagram post, Jennifer Lopez shared a cozy photo with Drake that broke the internet in 2016. Later, it was reported that the relationship broke off when she officially started seeing Alex Rodriguez. Drake even referenced her in two songs, Free Smoke and Diplomatic Immunity, and went on a trip to the Netherlands where he met Sophie Brussaux. J-Lo is a successful actress, singer, and businesswoman who earns millions from her album sales and brand endorsements.

    RELATED: JLo Celebrates 25 Years Since Her Most Important Movie

    1 Rihanna ($1.7 billion)

    One of the most popular personalities on the list, Rihanna needs no introduction. Drake and Rihanna go way back in 2005 when they first met while she was shooting for her music video Pon de Replay. They kept their relationship private in 2016 while they dated, and he confessed his love for Rihanna while presenting her the VMA Vanguard Award. With her music and a business venture, Fenty Beauty, Rihanna is the most successful female musician in the industry.

    Drake’s other rumored notable girlfriends include Bella Hadid, Hailey Bieber, and Kylie Jenner. While these were just rumors, Drake quickly clarified them before they became headlines on the internet. A true romantic at heart, the rapper's romances can be seen through his music.
