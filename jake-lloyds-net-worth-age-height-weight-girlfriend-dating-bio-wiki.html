    Early Life & Biography

    Jake Lloyd was born in Colorado, the U.S., on March 5, 1989, to parents William Lloyd and Lisa Riley. William Lloyd worked at the EMT, and Lisa Riley was an entertainment agent. He also has a sister named Madison. He graduated in 2007 from Carmel High School in Carmel, Indiana. He went to Columbia College in Chicago but couldn’t continue and left after a semester. He was studying film and psychology.

    Personal Life

    In 2012, Jake Lloyd explained his reasons to retire from acting in 2001. He said that he used to get bullied in school due to acting. As a result, he decided to destroy all his memorabilia from Star Wars.

    In 2015, there was an alleged assault by Jake on his mother. In a statement to the police, Lisa alleged that Lloyd arrived at his house and began berating her verbally. He then progressed to physically assault her, but she didn’t press charges because he was diagnosed with schizophrenia and was not taking his medicines.

    On June 17, 2015, Jake was arrested in South Carolina for reckless driving, driving without a license, and resisting arrest. He was under the name of Jake Broadbent. In April 2016, after being kept for ten months without trial, he was transferred from jail to a psychiatric facility because of his schizophrenia. In 2020, it was revealed that he has officially been diagnosed with paranoid schizophrenia.

    Age, Height, and Weight

    Being born on 5 March 1989, Jake Lloyd is 31 years old as of today’s date 24th January 2021. His height is 1.65m tall, and his weight is 79 kg.

    Career

    Jake Lloyd’s career started in 1996 when he got the role of Jimmy Sweet in ER. He also got a role in Unhook the Stars in the same year. It was beginning to feel like Jake was going to be the next big movie star. He got the role of Mark Armstrong in Apollo 11. He also acted in The Pretender. Then in 1999, his life changed.

    He got the role of Anakin Skywalker in Star Wars. Not only in the movie, but he also lent his voice to the character of Anakin Skywalker in the Star Wars video games from 1999 to 2002. He retired from acting in 2001 but continued to make appearances at sci-fi and comic book festivals.

    Awards & Achievements

    His biggest achievement would be getting the role of Anakin Skywalker in Star Wars. He got a Young Artist Award for that role.

    But not only that, he was even nominated for the Golden Raspberry Award as the Worst Supporting Actor, but he didn’t win it.
